About the Legend

Richard Darwin Cantos Jr., performing under the stage name Richie_Rich007, is a dynamic artist, songwriter, and composer hailing from Oceanside, California. Emerging from the punk revival scene, Richard crafts music that channels raw emotion, personal introspection, and the highs and lows of life over the past twenty years. His lyrics often explore themes of love, heartbreak, resilience, and daily struggles, delivered with an authentic edge that resonates with listeners seeking genuine storytelling.

Self-taught and self-produced, Richard began his musical journey in his teens, blending punk energy with melodic elements to create a sound that’s both haunting and invigorating. Influences from rock, folk, and ambient genres shine through in his compositions, featuring layered instrumentation, evocative vocals, and a DIY ethos that defines his independent spirit.

In 2026, Richard released his debut album, 2 Decades of Sorrow, a 16-track opus clocking in at over an hour, distributed via DistroKid and 11777215 Records DK. Released on February 19, the album includes standout tracks like “A Girl I Never Knew,” “Forever I’ll Love You,” “I Got This Girl I Know,” and “I Wake Up Everyday,” showcasing his talent for turning personal experiences into relatable anthems.

It’s now available on major platforms like Pandora, with promotional content highlighting its punk revival vibes. As Richie_Rich007, Richard connects with fans through social media, sharing snippets, full videos, and updates on Instagram (@richie_rich007_). With performances at local Southern California venues and collaborations on the horizon, he continues to build his presence in the indie music world, aiming to inspire through unfiltered expression and emotional depth.
