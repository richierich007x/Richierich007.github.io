About

Richie_Rich007 is an emo punk rock artist and songwriter based in Southern California. Blending raw emotional lyricism with high-energy punk instrumentation, the project captures the essence of two decades of lived experience—heartbreak, resilience, and the relentless pursuit of authentic expression.

"2 Decades of Sorrow" marks the debut full-length release, featuring 16 tracks that traverse themes of lost love, personal struggle, and eventual redemption. From the confessional opener "A Girl I Never Knew" to the haunting closer "Final Exit," the album is a journey through the shadows and back into the light.

For fans of Brand New, Taking Back Sunday, and the mid-2000s emo revival—reimagined for a new generation.
